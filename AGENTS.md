# Expo HAS CHANGED

Read the exact versioned docs at https://docs.expo.dev/versions/v56.0.0/ before writing any code.

  • Backgrounds:  background  (main screen),  card  (note cards, containers), and  surface  (inputs, modals, dialogs).
  • Borders:  border  (thin separator lines and borders).
  • Text:  text  (high-contrast title text),  textSecondary  (medium-contrast body/subtitles), and  textMuted  (low-contrast dates,
  placeholder hints).
  • Accents:  primary  (main brand highlight/action color) and  primaryContainer  (subtle active background states).
  • Statuses:  success ,  error , and  warning  for contextual messages or states.
